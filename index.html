Cosmetic Printing Materials

Beauty products demand materials that are skin-safe, color-stable, and premium in finish — our cosmetic printing portfolio meets all three.

  • ● SLA resin — high-gloss, smooth finish packaging
  • ● PolyJet — multi-color, multi-material compact shells
  • ● Nylon PA12 — durable matte-finish packaging
  • ● Flexible TPU — squeeze tubes and soft-touch grips
